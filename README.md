# Seed & Spoon NJ

**Nourishing Our Community, One Meal at a Time**

Seed & Spoon NJ is a nonprofit initiative dedicated to reducing food waste and fighting food insecurity in New Jersey. We connect surplus produce from local farms, grocery stores, and restaurants with families in need by providing freshly prepared meals through community-based distributions.

## Our Mission
- **Eliminate Food Waste:** Transform surplus food into nutritious meals.  
- **Combat Food Insecurity:** Deliver meals to schools, community centers, and families in need.  
- **Foster Community:** Build connections through volunteering, donations, and shared responsibility.

## How You Can Help
- **Donate:** Financial contributions help cover kitchen space, transportation, and ingredients.  
- **Volunteer:** Join our team in cooking, packing, and distributing meals.  
- **Support Our Wishlist:** Provide essential kitchen supplies through our [Amazon Wishlist](#).
